What are Tasks?

Tasks are things you want to get done like assignments, projects, chores, etc...

What are Rewards?

Rewards are things you want to let yourself do or get when you complete a task like order a pizza, watch Netflix for an hour, etc...

Why are the Tasks different colours?

The different colours signify different EXP amounts. The values are as follows:

  • Red - 50 EXP
  • Yellow - 40 EXP
  • Blue - 30 EXP
  • Green - 20 EXP
  • Black - 10 EXP

    • What do I do when I complete task?

    On the Tasks view hit the checkmark to the right of the task to complete it.

    How do I claim a reward?

    On the Rewards view hit the claim button beside the reward you want to claim.

    Why am I losing HP?

    This happens when a task's due date has passed. Do not let this happen!

    What happens if my HP goes to 0?

    You lose the game and you have to restart from the beginning.

    My Max HP decreases when I level up??

    The higher level you are, the more difficult the game gets and that is why your Max HP decreases when you level up.
